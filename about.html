About Japanese

The Japanese company was established in 2005, with our production facilities on a 1500 square meter.

The company stands among the leading companies in the field of Mold making, and as well, in the production of unusual spare parts due to the modern production methods and the latest of equipment used.

Our technical engineers and production staff are of a high caliber to fulfill our aim of furnishing the highest service possible to our clients who rightfully gave us their trust.

Our Mission

The Japanese company is committed to providing high standards in the manufacture of scaffolding according to international quality standards through a trained and professional team.

The company is keen to satisfy its customers through working mechanisms.

The Company firmly believes that the development of the Company is linked to its human resources.

The history of the Japanese experience that it has in the field of manufacturing to keep pace with the era of globalization.
